Areas of Practice Criminal Defence  ·  Sydney

Bail Applications

When charged and placed under arrest, you may be refused bail and brought before a court. We have extensive experience in first-instance bail applications in Local Courts across NSW, and in Supreme Court bail applications where first-instance bail is refused. First bail hearings are critical — there are limited circumstances in which a second application can be made.

Defended Hearings

We are accomplished advocates in defended Local Court hearings. We analyse complex briefs of evidence, identify the legal issues, and strategically challenge the prosecution case. We understand how important it is to convey your side of the story and prepare a thorough defence to each aspect of the allegations.

Trials

Serious criminal charges are defended in the District and Supreme Courts of NSW. We have defended clients accused of a wide range of offences with many resulting in acquittal. We work closely with senior and junior barristers and have a well-established network of experienced counsel we brief to defend criminal charges at trial.

Sentence Proceedings

When being sentenced, skilled representation ensures the court fully understands your personal circumstances. We advocate for the best possible outcome, ensuring all relevant factors are placed before the court — including good character, financial hardship, family circumstances, mental health and rehabilitation.

Appeals

We regularly appear in appellate matters in the District Court and Court of Criminal Appeal. We are experienced in reviewing lower court proceedings, identifying legal errors, and advising on appeal merits. We assist with preparing appeal paperwork, strategic advice, and appearing at the hearing.

Traffic Offences

A traffic offence carrying risk of licence loss or conviction can be very stressful. We appear across the full range of traffic matters — drink driving, drug driving, speeding, dangerous driving, licence disqualification — and fight for the best outcome in every case.

Apprehended Violence Orders

When an AVO is brought against you, it can prevent you from returning home or contacting family members. We represent defendants in AVO proceedings, negotiating conditions and duration, seeking property recovery orders, or contesting the application entirely.

Drug Offences

We represent individuals charged with the full range of drug offences — from simple possession through to supply, trafficking, and importation — in both State and Commonwealth jurisdictions. We bring a thorough, strategic approach and work closely with experienced counsel in serious drug trials.

Robbery & Theft

We represent individuals facing charges of robbery, armed robbery, aggravated robbery, break and enter, and theft offences across all levels of the NSW courts. These matters range from Local Court proceedings through to serious armed robbery trials in the District and Supreme Courts.

Sexual Offences

We provide sensitive, thorough representation for individuals charged with sexual offences. These are often complex matters requiring careful preparation and experienced advocacy. We approach every sexual offence matter with the gravity it deserves and ensure our clients receive skilled defence at every stage.

Murder & Manslaughter

We have experience in serious homicide matters, including murder and manslaughter, before the NSW Supreme Court. These are the most serious criminal charges a person can face. We work closely with King’s Counsel and Senior Counsel to ensure our clients receive the highest level of representation.

Fraud & Dishonesty

We act in a wide range of fraud and dishonesty matters, including obtaining financial advantage by deception, identity fraud, and Centrelink fraud. We also appear in proceeds of crime applications and Australian Crime Commission matters involving the recovery of alleged criminal proceeds.

Money Laundering

Money laundering offences under State and Commonwealth law involve complex legal and evidentiary issues. We have experience in serious money laundering matters, proceeds of crime applications, and the confiscation of assets under the Criminal Assets Recovery Act.

Domestic Violence

Domestic violence charges and AVO proceedings are interconnected and require careful, coordinated management. We advise on the interplay between criminal charges and protection orders, appear in both streams of proceedings, and work to protect our clients’ interests at every stage.

Common Assault & ABH

We defend individuals charged with common assault and assault occasioning actual bodily harm in the Local Court and District Court. These matters require careful preparation, thorough cross-examination, and a clear strategy whether proceeding to a defended hearing or negotiating a favourable plea.

Grievous Bodily Harm

GBH and wounding offences under s 33 and s 35 of the Crimes Act 1900 are serious indictable matters typically prosecuted in the District Court. We have experience in the full range of serious violence matters and work closely with senior counsel in complex trials.

Break & Enter

Break and enter offences carry significant maximum penalties in NSW. We represent defendants in both Local Court and District Court proceedings, from aggravated break and enter through to dwelling break and enter matters, with a focus on achieving the best available outcome for each client.

Stalking & Intimidation

Stalking and intimidation charges under s 13 of the Crimes (Domestic and Personal Violence) Act 2007 are prosecuted in the Local Court and, for serious matters, the District Court. These charges often arise alongside AVO proceedings and require a coordinated defence across both matters.

Possess & Use Prohibited Drugs

Simple drug possession and use charges under the Drug Misuse and Trafficking Act 1985 are among the most common matters in NSW Local Courts. We provide practical, results-focused advice and representation, whether proceeding to a defended hearing, negotiating a section 10 outcome, or seeking a diversion.

Firearms Offences

Firearms offences carry serious mandatory minimum sentences in NSW. We represent clients charged with unauthorised possession, use, and trafficking of firearms. Early and skilled legal advice is critical in these matters given the significant sentencing consequences.
